<section class="home-section profile-story">
  <div class="section-heading">
    <p class="eyebrow">Profile</p>
    <h2>Research with academic depth and field relevance</h2>
  </div>

  <div class="story-grid">
    <div class="story-grid__main">
      <p>I am an engineer, researcher, and educator working at the intersection of construction engineering, machine learning, and sustainability. My goal is to build methods that do more than perform well in theory. They need to support better decisions in buildings, materials, and infrastructure.</p>
      <p>Across my projects, I use tools such as XGBoost, LSTM models, EnergyPlus, Rhino and Grasshopper, ifcopenshell, and life cycle assessment workflows to turn fragmented project data into useful insight. I care about systems that are rigorous enough for research and practical enough for adoption.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="story-grid__aside">
      <div class="fact-card">
        <p class="hero-panel__label">Current Focus</p>
        <p>Retrofit forecasting for urban housing, automated energy workflows from IFC, and carbon accounting in timber construction.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="fact-card">
        <p class="hero-panel__label">Methods</p>
        <p>Python, Machine Learning, Energy Modeling, BIM, IFC, LCA, SQL, GIS, and computational design workflows.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
